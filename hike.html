Seven Mountains, Bergen

duration: 4 days, Norway

Hiking

Mountains

Featured

The "Seven Mountains" are the peaks surrounding the Norwegian city of Bergen. The Bergen Hiking Club offers an annual tour of these. Be sure to bring rain gear!

Monte Capanne, Tuscany

duration: 4 days, Norway

Hiking

Mountains

Snow

Located on the island of Elba, is one of the highest peaks in Tuscany. Visit the Hermitage of San Cerbone, as well as the fort near the medieval village.
